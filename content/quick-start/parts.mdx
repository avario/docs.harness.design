# Create and Assign Parts

<Steps>
### Open the Parts view
Click the view drop down to change to the Parts view
<br />
<img src="/quick-start/parts-view-open.png" width={256} style={{ borderRadius: '8px' }}/>

### Add a [Part](/parts)
Right click or click on the "+" at the bottom left of the parts screen and select a part from the dropdown menu
<br />
<img src="/quick-start/right-add-part.png" width={147} style={{ borderRadius: '8px' }}/>

### Edit Part properties
Click the arrow on the right hand side of the part screen table to adjust the properties of parts
<br />
<img src="/quick-start/edit-part.png" width={634} style={{ borderRadius: '8px' }}/>

### Assigning Parts
Right click on a Component in either the Schematic, Layout or Connection view and select the part you want to assign
<br />
<img src="/quick-start/assign-part.png" width={634} style={{ borderRadius: '8px' }}/>

</Steps>
