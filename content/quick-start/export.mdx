# Export

<Steps>
### Export Wiring Table and BOM
To export go Main Menu > Export and select Wiring table or BOM to export as .csv. This can be opened in any spreadsheet application.
<br />
<img src="/quick-start/export-menu.png" width={381} style={{ borderRadius: '8px' }}/>

### Export Layout
Click the view drop down to change to the Layout view
<br />
<img src="/quick-start/layout-view-open.png" width={256} style={{ borderRadius: '8px' }}/>

Click on "View options" at the bottom right of the Layout view and select "Show covering part details" 
<br />
<img src="/quick-start/layout-export-show.png" width={311} style={{ borderRadius: '8px' }}/>

Click the dowload button at the bottom right of the Layout view to export the Layout as a .png
<br />
<img src="/quick-start/layout-export.png" width={216} style={{ borderRadius: '8px' }}/>

### Export Schematic
Click the view drop down to change to the Schematic view
<br />
<img src="/quick-start/schematic-view-open.png" width={256} style={{ borderRadius: '8px' }}/>

Click on "View options" at the bottom right of the Schematic view and select "Always show wire parts" 
<br />
<img src="/quick-start/schematic-export-show.png" width={311} style={{ borderRadius: '8px' }}/>

Click the dowload button at the bottom right of the Schematic view to export the Schematic as a .png
<br />
<img src="/quick-start/schematic-export.png" width={216} style={{ borderRadius: '8px' }}/>

</Steps>
