# Review Connections

<Steps>
### Open the Connections View  
Click the view dropdown and select **Connections**.  
<br />  
<img src="/quick-start/connection-view-open.png" width={256} style={{ borderRadius: '8px' }} />

### Review Connections  
The connections table lists all wires in your harness, including their source and destination. Wire lengths are automatically calculated based on the bundles they pass through.  
<br />  
<img src="/quick-start/connection-table.png" width={634} style={{ borderRadius: '8px' }} />
</Steps>

### Connections View, View Options

  - **Show IDs instead of labels** will change the From and To columns to show either the component label or the automatically generated component ID.
  - **Show contacts** will show the contact a connection is terminated with if applicable.
  - The remaining options allow you to hide and show specific columns in the connections table.
