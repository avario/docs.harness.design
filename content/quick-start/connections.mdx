# Review Connections

<Steps>
### Open the <IconLine/> [Connections](/view/connections) View  
Click the view dropdown and select **Connections**.  
<br />  
<img src="/quick-start/connection-view-open.png" width={256} style={{ borderRadius: '8px' }} />

### Review Connections  
The connections table lists all [Wires](/component/wire) in your harness, including their source and destination. Wire lengths are automatically calculated based on the [Bundles](/component/bundle) they pass through.
