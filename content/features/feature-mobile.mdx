# Mobile Support

Mobile devices are supported in read-only mode. Perfect for when you are on the shop floor or at the racetrack and need to troubleshoot your harness.

<br />
<img src="/features/mobile-preview.png" width={800} style={{ borderRadius: "8px" }} />

## Opening a Harness on Mobile

Go to [app.harness.design](https://app.harness.design) and log in to your account. Goto <IconMenu2/> **Menu** → <IconFolder/> **Open...** and select the harness you would like to view or simply open a public link shared with you.
