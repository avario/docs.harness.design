## Make your Harness Public

Toggle **Make public** to allow anyone with a link to view your harness.

## Share your Harness with an Email

Enter an email and click **Send Invite** to allow the user with that email address to view your harness.

An email will be sent to the address with a link to view the harness. They will need to create an account if they don't already have one.

## Revoke Email Access

Click the Revoke access <IconCircleMinus /> button next to an email to revoke their access to view your harness.

<br />

## Viewing Harnesses Shared with You

To open a harness shared with you by email, select the **Shared with You** tab in the **Open** window.
