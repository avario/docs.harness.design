# Splice

A <IconSplice/> `Splice` connects two or more wires within the harness. Each **Splice** can be assigned a [splice part](/parts/part-splice).

## Adding a Splice

`right-click` on the canvas in the [Schematic View](/quick-start/schematic) and select <IconSplice/> `Splice`.  
<br />  
<img src="/components/splice-add-right-click.png" width={634} style={{ borderRadius: '8px' }} />

You can also add a **Splice** by selecting multiple wires in the Schematic View, then `right-click` and choosing <IconSplice/> `Add splice`.  
<br />  
<img src="/components/splice-add-wires.png" width={634} style={{ borderRadius: '8px' }} />

## Locating a Splice

**Splices** are automatically positioned based on the most efficient wiring path. To position a **Splice** manually, drag its location handle in the **Layout** view to any [Branch Point](/components/component-branch-point), [Connector](/components/component-connector), or [Terminal](/components/component-terminal).  
<br />  
<img src="/components/splice-locate-manual.png" width={634} style={{ borderRadius: '8px' }} />

<Callout>  
To reset a **Splice** to automatic placement, select its location line and press `Delete`.
</Callout>
