# Net Label

A net label assigns a name to a net of wires. This is useful for the following reasons:
- Labels on connector cavities are auto-filled with the **Net Label** name.
- If two nets should be the same, adding a **Net Label** with the same name will merge them.
- Connections on the Connection View that are on a net with a **Net Label** will show the **Net Label** name making it easier to keep track of wires.

## When to use a Net Label
Use a **Net Label** for larger nets that span multiple components or connectors. Often this is used for power nets (e.g. VCC, GND) or common signal nets (e.g. I2C, SPI).

## Adding a Net Label
In the **Schematic View**, right click on the canvas and select **Net Label**.
<br />  
<img src="/components/net-label-add.png" width={164} style={{ borderRadius: '8px' }} />

## Assigning a Name to a Net Label
Double click on the **Net Label** to edit its name.  
<br />  
<img src="/components/net-label-name.png" width={130} style={{ borderRadius: '8px' }} />
<Callout>  
If a **Net Label** shares the same name as another **Net Label**, the nets will be connected together.
</Callout>

## Assigning a Color to a Net Label
Right-click on the **Net Label** select **Set Color** and choose a color.
<br />  
<img src="/components/net-label-color.png" width={329} style={{ borderRadius: '8px' }} />

## Assigning a Net Label to a Net
Drag from the handle on the left or right side of the **Net Label** to the end of a wire or cavity to assign the **Net Label** to that net.
<br />  
<img src="/components/net-label-assign.png" width={471} style={{ borderRadius: '8px' }} />

## Net Label in Connections View
**Net Labels** are shown on all connections in the **Connections View** that belong to a net with a **Net Label**.
<br />  
<img src="/components/net-label-connections.png" width={878} style={{ borderRadius: '8px' }} />

## Net Label in Connector Cavities
The **Net Label** is shown in the cavity label of any connector cavity that is connected to a net with a **Net Label**.
