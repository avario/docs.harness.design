# Connector
A connector joins electrical parts, allowing easy connection and disconnection of circuits. Connectors contain contacts. They are termination points of the harness

## Add a Connector
Right click on the artboard on the Schematic or Layout View to add a Connector
<br />
<img src="/components/connector-add.png" width={634} style={{ borderRadius: '8px' }}/>

## Locate a Connector
Drag a connector by clicking and dragging it in the Layout View
<br />
<img src="/components/connector-locate.png" width={634} style={{ borderRadius: '8px' }}/>

## Add cavities to a Connector
Select a connector in the Schematic view and click the "+" or "-" buttons to add or remove cavities
