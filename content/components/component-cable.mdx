# Cable
A cable is a group of wires wrapped in a sheath

## Add a Cable
Right click on the artboard of the Schematic view to add a Cable
<br />
<img src="/components/cable-add.png" width={634} style={{ borderRadius: '8px' }}/>

Select multiple Wires and right click and select "Make Cable" to merge the wires into a cable
<br />
<img src="/components/cable-add-wires.png" width={634} style={{ borderRadius: '8px' }}/>

## Assign a [Cable Part](/parts/part-cable)
Right click on a Cable in the Schematic View and select the Cable Part you want to assign
<br />
<img src="/components/cable-assign.png" width={634} style={{ borderRadius: '8px' }}/>

## Route a Cable
Cables are routed automatically based on connections and bundle locations. Cable length is based on longest wire
<br />
<img src="/components/cable-route.png" width={634} style={{ borderRadius: '8px' }}/>

## Add wires to Cable

Select the Cable in the Schematic View and click the "+" button to add wires
<br />
<img src="/components/cable-add-wire.png" width={634} style={{ borderRadius: '8px' }}/>

## Add sheild to Cable

Right click the Cable in the Schematic View and click "Add Sheild"
<br />
<img src="/components/cable-sheild.png" width={634} style={{ borderRadius: '8px' }}/>
