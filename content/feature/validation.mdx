# <IconCopyCheck/>Validation

harness.design has several built-in tools to make sure your harness is built correctly.

To use the validation tools make sure warnings are enabled by `left-clicking` the <IconExclamationCircle/>**Show warnings** button at the bottom right of the [Schematic](/view/schematic) or [Layout](/view/layout) views.

## Wire Gauge
If the [Wire](/part/wire) gauge does not match the properties of the [Contact](/part/accessory/contact), a warning is shown in the Schematic view.  
<br />  
<img src="/feature/validate-guage.png" width={432} style={{ borderRadius: '8px' }} />  
If multiple wires go into one contact, the sizes will be summed to calculate the total gauge.  
<br />  
<img src="/feature/validate-guage-multiple.png" width={397} style={{ borderRadius: '8px' }} />  
<Callout>  
Multiple wire gauges are calculated by converting all units to `mm²`, adding them together, then converting back to the harness's default gauge unit.  
</Callout>

## Connector Cavities
If the [Connector](/component/connector) and [Connector Part](/part/connector) do not match the number of cavities, a warning is shown in the Schematic view.  
<br />  
<img src="/feature/validate-cavities.png" width={262} style={{ borderRadius: '8px' }} />  
Parts with the same cavity count are suggested at the top of the parts list when assigning a part to a connector.  
<br />  
<img src="/feature/validate-cavities-suggested.png" width={508} style={{ borderRadius: '8px' }} />

## Contact Compatibility
[Contacts](/part/accessory/contact) from the same manufacturer are suggested at the top of the parts list when assigning a contact to a connector.  
<br />  
<img src="/feature/validate-contacts.png" width={572} style={{ borderRadius: '8px' }} />

## Unassigned Wires
[Wires](/component/wire) with no part assigned to them are highlighted in the Connection view as `No Part`.  
<br />  
<img src="/feature/validate-wire-part.png" width={800} style={{ borderRadius: '8px' }} />

## Unrouted Wires
[Wires](/component/wire) that cannot be routed through [Bundles](/component/bundle) will show a `NO ROUTE` warning on the Connection screen.
<br />  
<img src="/feature/validate-wire-route.png" width={800} style={{ borderRadius: '8px' }} />

## Unassigned Parts
[Parts](/part) that have not been assigned to components are highlighted in the Parts view as `Not Used`.  
<br />  
<img src="/feature/validate-parts.png" width={800} style={{ borderRadius: '8px' }} />

## Connector and Terminal Mates
Terminals and Connectors are validated to ensure that they are compatible mates. See:
- [Mating Connectors](/feature/mates#mating-connectors)
- [Mating Terminals](/feature/mates#mating-terminals)
