# <IconUsersGroup/>Teams<EnterpriseBadge/>

Teams let you collaborate on harnesses with your colleagues — share harnesses across the team and edit them together.

Teams are managed from <IconMenu2/>**Main Menu** → <IconUserCircle/>**Account** → <IconUsersGroup/>**Team**.

<br />
<img src="/feature/team.png" width={600} style={{ borderRadius: "8px" }} />

<Callout>
  Teams are an **Enterprise** feature. You need an active Enterprise subscription to create a team,
  and each member needs an Enterprise **seat** to edit the team's harnesses.
</Callout>

## How teams work

- A team has an **owner** (the person who created it) and any number of **members**.
- The team has a pool of **Enterprise seats**, provided by the owner's Enterprise subscription. A seat gives a member full Enterprise access and lets them edit the team's harnesses.
- You can belong to **one team** at a time.
