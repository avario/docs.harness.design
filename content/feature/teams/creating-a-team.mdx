# Creating a Team

Open <IconMenu2/>**Main Menu** → <IconUserCircle/>**Account** → <IconUsersGroup/>**Team** and create your team by giving it a name.

<Callout>
  Creating a team requires an active **Enterprise** subscription. If you're not on Enterprise, the
  Team tab prompts you to upgrade.
</Callout>

When you create a team:

- You become the team **owner**.
- You're automatically allocated one of your Enterprise **seats** — the owner always holds a seat.
- The number of seats available to your team matches the quantity on your Enterprise subscription.

From here you can [invite members](/feature/teams/inviting-members) and [move harnesses into the team](/feature/teams/collaborating).

<Callout type="warning">
  Deleting a team (from the same Team tab) removes it permanently. Members lose any Enterprise
  access that came from their seat. The team's harnesses return to their original owners — they're
  no longer shared with the team, so other members can't access them any more.
</Callout>
