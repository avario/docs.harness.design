# Seats

A **seat** grants a team member full **Enterprise** access — including the ability to edit the team's harnesses. Your team's seats come from the quantity on the owner's Enterprise subscription.

## Allocating and unassigning seats

In the <IconUsersGroup/>**Team** tab, the seat counter shows how many seats are allocated out of your total pool. Use the <IconDotsVertical/>**options** menu next to a member to **Assign seat** or **Unassign seat**.

- The **owner** always holds a seat and can't release it while subscribed to Enterprise.
- Seats reserved by [pending invitations](/feature/teams/inviting-members) count against the pool too.
- You can't allocate more seats than your subscription provides — add seats from **Manage seats & billing** if you need more.

## What members see

A member **with** a seat has access to Enterprise features everywhere they use harness.design and can open and edit the team's harnesses. A member **without** a seat can still see the team and its harnesses, but those harnesses are **read-only** for them until the owner assigns them a seat.
