# <IconTopologyRing3/> Nets

Select any part or component to quickly visualize where it is used across the views and the net it is connected to.

<Callout>
  Toggle <IconEye /> `View Options` → <IconBolt /> `Show nets for selection` to show the entire net
  or just individual components.
</Callout>

Selecting a [Connector](/component/connector) will show all wires it is connected to and their path through the harness.
