# <IconShare2/>Sharing

To let other people view your harness, go to <IconMenu2/>**Main Menu** → <IconShare2/>**Share**.

<br />
<img src="/view/main-menu/share.png" width={400} style={{ borderRadius: "8px" }} />

<Callout>
  Only the harness owner can edit the harness. Anyone the harness is shared with will only be able
  to view it in read-only mode.
</Callout>

## Harness Link

If the harness is not public, only users shared via email will be able to open this link. Click the <IconCopy/>**copy** button to copy the link to your clipboard.

## Make your Harness Public

Toggle to make your harness public. Anyone with the link will be able to view your harness as read-only.

## Embedding

Once your harness is public, you can embed an interactive copy of it on another website — this is how the live diagram on our [home page](https://harness.design) works. Click <IconCode/>**Embed** in the Share dialog to open the embed settings.

<Callout>
  The <IconCode /> **Embed** button is disabled until the harness is
  [public](#make-your-harness-public).
</Callout>

<br />
<img src="/feature/sharing-embed.png" width={700} style={{ borderRadius: "8px" }} />

As you adjust the settings, the **Link** and **Embed code** on the right update in real time. Copy the <IconCopy/>**Embed code** and paste the `<iframe>` snippet into your site, or copy the **Link** to open the demo directly.

Embedded harnesses open in **demo mode** — visitors can explore the harness, but any edits they make are never saved. They pan by dragging the canvas and zoom with the on-canvas controls (or with scroll gestures, if you've enabled them).

[Click here](/feature/sharing/embedded) to see an example of this in action.

### Embed Settings

- **Show harness title** — show or hide the title bar.
- **Views** — which views (Schematic, Layout, Parts, Connections) are shown when the embed first loads. At least one is required.
- **View options** — the on-canvas toggles (show parts, coverings, highlight nets, component IDs, warnings, and so on) and whether each is enabled by default.
- **Show view options** — whether visitors can change those view options themselves.
- **Color scheme** — display the embed in **Light**, **Dark**, or **Auto** (matching the visitor's device).
- **Interaction** — control what visitors can do with the embed:
  - **Allow interaction** — when turned off, the embed is a static, non-interactive image (no panning, zooming, or selecting) and the options below are disabled.
  - **Allow editing** — let visitors modify the harness (changes are never saved).
  - **Allow view switching** — show the view selector so visitors can change views.
  - **Allow search** — show the search control.
  - **Capture scroll gestures for pan/zoom** — when off (the default), scrolling over the embed scrolls the surrounding page instead of panning or zooming the harness. Turn it on to let scroll gestures control the canvas.
  - **Show zoom controls** — show zoom in/out buttons on the canvas (on by default).

## Email Sharing<ProBadge/>

Enter an email address and click `Send Invite` to allow the user with that email address to view your harness.

An email will be sent to the address with a link to view the harness. They will need to create an account if they don't already have one.

<Callout>
  Inviting people by email requires a Pro or Enterprise plan. On the Free plan the invite form is
  replaced by an upgrade prompt — but you can still see any existing email shares and **revoke**
  them, and those shares keep working.
</Callout>

## Revoke Email Access

Click <IconCircleMinus/>**Revoke access** next to an email to revoke a user's access to view your harness.
