# <IconShare2/> Sharing

To let other people view your harness, go to <IconMenu2 /> `Main Menu` → <IconShare2 /> `Share`.

<br />
<img src="/view/main-menu/share.png" width={400} style={{ borderRadius: "8px" }} />

<Callout>
  Only the harness owner can edit the harness. Anyone the harness is shared with will only be able
  to view it in read-only mode.
</Callout>

## Harness Link

If the harness is not public, only users shared via email will be able to open this link. Click the <IconCopy/> `copy` button to copy the link to your clipboard.

## Make your Harness Public

Toggle to make your harness public. Anyone with the link will be able to view your harness as read-only.

## Email Sharing

Enter an email address and click `Send Invite` to allow the user with that email address to view your harness.

An email will be sent to the address with a link to view the harness. They will need to create an account if they don't already have one.

## Revoke Email Access

Click <IconCircleMinus /> `Revoke access` next to an email to revoke a user's access to view your harness.
