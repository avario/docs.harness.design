# <IconDeviceMobile/> Mobile Support

Mobile devices are supported in read-only mode. Perfect for when you are on the shop floor or at the racetrack and need to troubleshoot your harness.

<img src="/feature/mobile-preview.png" width={800} style={{ borderRadius: "8px" }} />

## Opening a Harness on Mobile

Go to [app.harness.design](https://app.harness.design) and log in to your account. Goto <IconMenu2/> `Main Menu` → <IconFolder/> `Open...` and select the harness you would like to view or simply open a [public link](/view/main-menu/share#-make-your-harness-public) shared with you.
