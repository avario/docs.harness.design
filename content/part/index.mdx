# Parts

**Parts** are the physical items used in the harness—such as connectors, cables, and coverings. They are created and managed in the **Parts View**.

Each part can have specific properties like part number, manufacturer, colour, and more. Parts are assigned to [components](/component), and once assigned, relevant properties are automatically validated. For example, a [connector part](/part/connector) must have the same number of cavities as the [connector component](/component/connector) it is assigned to.

## Adding Parts

In the Parts View, `right-click` on the canvas or click the <IconPlus/> `Add part` button in the bottom-left corner to add a new part.

<br />
<img src="/part/part-add.png" width={222} style={{ borderRadius: "8px" }} />

## Creating Part from Component

Parts can be created by `right-click` a component in the **Schematic** or **Layout View** and selecting `Part` → <IconSquareRoundedPlus/> `Create New Part`.

<br />
<img src="/part/part-add-from-component.png" width={535} style={{ borderRadius: "8px" }} />

## Importing Parts

You can import parts from any of your existing projects by using the <IconFileImport/> `Import Parts` button in the **Parts View**.

Select the project you want to import from, then choose the parts you want to import. The selected parts will be added to your current project. This will not affect the original parts in the project you are importing from.

<br />
<img src="/part/part-import.png" width={634} style={{ borderRadius: "8px" }} />

<Callout type="warning">
  You will see a warning if any part numbers of the imported parts conflict with existing parts.
</Callout>

<Callout>
**Part Library Workaround**

1. Create a separate harness design and call it `Master Parts Library` or similar
2. Add Parts to the [Parts View](/view/parts) in this harness
3. In the harness you want to add parts to simply import them from the `Master Parts Library` harness

**Note:** You can have multiple tabs of **harness.design** open at once. This means you can have your `Master Parts Library` harness and current harness open at the same time

</Callout>

## Editing Parts

`left-double-click` on a part name or click the <IconEdit/> button to view and edit a part's properties.

<br />
<img src="/part/part-edit.png" width={634} style={{ borderRadius: "8px" }} />

## Assigning Parts

Parts are assigned to components in the [Layout](/quick-start/layout) or [Schematic](/quick-start/schematic) view. `right-click` a component, then select the appropriate part from the list.

<br />
<img src="/component/connector-assign.png" width={634} style={{ borderRadius: "8px" }} />

<Callout>Quantities are automatically updated as parts are assigned to components.</Callout>

## Part Price

Part price can be entered by double clicking a part to edit it properties and setting the **Price** value. Parts that have a length can be assigned a length unit.

Total price of the individual parts and entire harness can be seen on the [Parts](/view/parts) view. Enable the **Unit Price** and **Total Price** under the <IconEye/>`View Options` menu on the **Parts** view.

<br />
<img src="/part/part-price.png" width={600} style={{ borderRadius: "8px" }} />

<Callout>
  You can change the currency symbol used in the document by going to <IconMenu2 />
  `Main Menu` → <IconSettings />
  `Settings` → <IconFile />
  `Harness settings` → `Currency` and selecting the desired symbol. [Learn
  more](/view/main-menu/settings#currency)
</Callout>

## Removing Parts from BOM

Parts can be removed from the BOM by `right-clicking` a component in the **Layout** or **Schematic View** with a part assigned and deselecting <IconNut/>`Include in BOM`.

<br />
<img src="/part/part-remove-bom.png" width={307} style={{ borderRadius: "8px" }} />

## Unassigned Parts

To show components with unassigned parts, in the <IconEye/> `View Options` at the bottom right of the Layout and Schematic Views select <IconAlertHexagon/> `Unassigned part warnings`.

- `Red warning symbol` No assigned part
- `Orange warning symbol` No assigned part (Excluded from BOM)
