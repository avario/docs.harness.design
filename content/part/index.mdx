# Parts

**Parts** are the physical items used in the harness—such as connectors, cables, and coverings. They are created and managed in the **Parts View**.

Each part can have specific properties like part number, manufacturer, colour, and more. Parts are assigned to [components](/component), and once assigned, relevant properties are automatically validated. For example, a [connector part](/part/connector) must have the same number of cavities as the [connector component](/component/connector) it is assigned to.

## Adding Parts

In the Parts View, `right-click` on the canvas or click the <IconPlus/>`Add part` button in the bottom-left corner to add a new part.

<br />
<img src="/part/part-add.png" width={222} style={{ borderRadius: "8px" }} />

## Creating Part from Component

Parts can be created by `right-click` a component in the **Schematic** or **Layout View** and selecting <IconNut/>`Assign Part` -> <IconHexagonPlus/>`Create new part`.

<br />
<img src="/part/part-add-from-component.png" width={535} style={{ borderRadius: "8px" }} />

## Editing Parts

`left-double-click` on a part name or `right-click` the part and select  the <IconPencil />`Edit` to view and edit a part's properties.

<br />
<img src="/part/part-edit.png" width={634} style={{ borderRadius: "8px" }} />

## Assigning Parts

`right-click` on a component in the [Schematic](/view/schematic) or [Layout](/view/layout) and select <IconNut/>`Assign Part`. Search for or select that part you would like to assign. The quantities shown in the [Parts](/view/parts) View will be automatically updated as you assign parts.

<br />  
<video src="/part/assign-part.mp4" width={634} style={{ borderRadius: '8px' }} autoPlay loop muted playsInline />

<Callout>
  The visibility of parts can be toggled on the **Layout** and **Schematic** screens by `left-clicking` <IconNut/>`Show parts` in the bottom right hand corner of the view.
</Callout>

## Assigning Coverings

`right-click` on a component in the [Schematic](/view/schematic) or [Layout](/view/layout) and select <IconCylinder style={{transform: 'rotate(90deg)'}}/>`Add covering`. Search for or select that covering you would like to assign. The quantities shown in the [Parts](/view/parts) View will be automatically updated as you assign parts.

<br />  
<video src="/part/assign-covering.mp4" width={634} style={{ borderRadius: '8px' }} autoPlay loop muted playsInline />

<Callout>
  The visibility of parts can be toggled on the **Layout** and **Schematic** screens by `left-clicking` <IconCylinder style={{transform: 'rotate(90deg)'}}/>`Show coverings` in the bottom right hand corner of the view.
</Callout>

## Part Price

Part price can be entered by double clicking a part to edit it properties and setting the **Price** value. Parts that have a length can be assigned a length unit.

Total price of the individual parts and entire harness can be seen on the [Parts](/view/parts) view. Show the **Unit Price** and **Total Price** under the <IconColumns3/>`Columns` menu.

<br />
<img src="/part/part-price.png" width={600} style={{ borderRadius: "8px" }} />

<Callout>
  You can change the currency symbol used in the document by going to <IconMenu2/>`Main Menu` → <IconSettings/>`Settings` → <IconFile/>`Harness settings` → `Currency` and selecting the desired symbol. [Learn
  more](/view/main-menu/settings#currency)
</Callout>

## Excluding Parts from BOM

Parts can be removed from the BOM by `right-clicking` a component in the **Layout** or **Schematic View** with a part assigned and selecting <IconReceiptOff/>`Exclude from BOM`.
