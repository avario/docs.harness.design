# Covering Part
A covering part is used to wrap or cover a [Bundle](/component/bundle) or [Branch Point](/component/branch-point). These parts are applied and can be seen in the **Layout View**.

There are several types of coverings supported by harness.design:

- [Heatshrink](/part/covering/heatshrink) - A heat-activated sleeve that shrinks to conform tightly around wires or bundles for insulation and strain relief.
- [Tape](/part/covering/tape) - A flexible adhesive or self-fusing tape wrapped around wires or bundles for protection and bundling.
- [Corrugated Tubing](/part/covering/corrugated-tubing) - A ribbed, flexible conduit that protects wire bundles from abrasion and allows routing flexibility.
- [Spiral Wrap](/part/covering/spiral-wrap) - A helically wound strip that wraps around a bundle for abrasion protection while allowing branch-outs.
- [Tubing](/part/covering/tubing) - A smooth-walled flexible sleeve that encloses a wire bundle for protection and organisation.
- [Braided Sleeve](/part/covering/braided-sleeve) - An expandable woven sleeve that provides flexible, high-coverage protection for wire bundles.
