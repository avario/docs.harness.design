# <IconCavitySeal/> Cavity Seal Part

A small seal—often a rubber grommet—fitted around an individual [Contact](/part/accessory/contact) where its wire enters a connector cavity. It provides environmental sealing against moisture and dust on a per-contact basis.

## Assigning a Cavity Seal

A cavity seal is not assigned to a component directly. Instead it is assigned to a [Contact Part](/part/accessory/contact), in the same way that other accessories are assigned through a [Connector Configuration](/part/connector#connector-configurations).

To assign a cavity seal, edit a [Contact Part](/part/accessory/contact) and `left-click` the <IconNut/>**Cavity Seal** field, then select or create the seal you want to associate with that contact.
