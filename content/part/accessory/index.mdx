# Connector Accessories
Accessories are parts that attach to a [Connector](/part/connector) housing to provide additional functionality such as electrical termination, sealing, retention, and strain relief.

There are several types of connector accessories supported by harness.design:

- [Contact](/part/accessories/contact) - A conductive element inserted into a connector cavity to establish an electrical connection with a mating contact.
- [Lock](/part/accessories/lock) - A secondary locking device that prevents contact backout and ensures reliable retention within the connector housing.
- [Dust Cover](/part/accessories/dust-cover) - A protective cap fitted to an unmated connector to prevent contamination and protect the contacts.
- [Boot](/part/accessories/boot) - A flexible seal fitted to the rear of a connector for strain relief and environmental protection of the cable entry.
- [Backshell](/part/accessories/backshell) - A rigid or semi-rigid enclosure fitted to the rear of a connector for cable termination, strain relief, shielding, and environmental protection.
