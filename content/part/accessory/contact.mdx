# <IconContact/> Contact Part
A conductive element installed in a connector housing that forms the electrical interface between mating connectors. This is often crimped or soldered to a wire and has a specific shape to fit into a corresponding connector housing.

## Assigning Contacts

Contacts can be assigned through a [Connector Configuration](/part/connector#connector-configurations) and are automatically applied to any [Connector Component](/component/connector) when that [Connector Part](/part/connector) is assigned.

## Manually Assigning Contacts

To manually assign or override contacts, `left-click` the <IconNut/> terminal part button in the connector cavity within the Schematic View and select the desired terminal.
