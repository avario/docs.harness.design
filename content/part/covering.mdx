# Covering Part
A covering part is used to wrap or cover a [Bundle](/component/bundle) or [Branch Point](/component/branch-point). These parts are applied and can be seen in the **Layout View**.

## Covering Types

- `Heatshrink`  
- `Tape`  
- `Corrugated Tubing`
- `Spiral Wrap`
- `Tubing`
- `Braided Sleeve`

## Assigning a Covering Part

`right-click` a [Bundle](/component/bundle) or [Branch Point](/component/branch-point) in the **Layout View** and select `Coverings` and select the desired covering part you wish to assign. You can assign multiple coverings to a single **Bundle** or **Branch Point**.
