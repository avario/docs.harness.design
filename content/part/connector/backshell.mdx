# Backshell Part
A rigid or semi-rigid enclosure fitted to the rear of a connector for cable termination, strain relief, shielding, and environmental protection.

## Assigning a Backshell Part

`right-click` on a connector in the **Schematic** or **Layout View**, click `Accessories` → `Backshell` and select the backshell part you want to assign.
