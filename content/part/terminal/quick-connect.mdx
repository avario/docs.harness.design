# <IconQuickConnect/> Quick Connect Part
The male quick connect has a flat, blade-like end that can be inserted into a female quick connect. Quick connects allow for fast, tool-free connections and are commonly used in automotive and appliance wiring.

<Callout>
For size, you can enter the following units (set the default unit under <IconMenu2/> `Main Menu` → <IconSettings/> `Settings` → <IconFile/> `Harness settings`):
  - `mm` - Milimeter
  - `cm` - Centimeter
  - `m` - Meter
  - `in` `"` - Inch
  - `ft` `'` - Foot
  - `yd` - Yard
  - `other` - You can enter strings for other usecase i.e. #8 for US Screw Gauge
</Callout>

<Callout>
For gauge, you can enter the following units (set the default unit under <IconMenu2/> `Main Menu` → <IconSettings/> `Settings` → <IconFile/> `Harness settings`):
  - `mm` - Milimeters Squared (mm²)
  - `awg` - American Wire Gauge (AWG)
</Callout>
