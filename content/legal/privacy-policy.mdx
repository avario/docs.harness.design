# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** 19 January 2026

harness.design is operated from New Zealand. This Privacy Policy explains how personal information and user-generated content are collected, used, stored, and protected when you use the website or service. It includes information for users in the European Union (GDPR) and California (CCPA).

## 1. Information we collect
We may collect the following information:
- Name or username  
- Email address  
- Account credentials  
- Usage data such as pages visited, features used, timestamps  
- Technical data such as IP address, browser type, device type  
- User-generated content including wiring harness designs and related project data  

We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal information.

## 2. User-generated content
The service allows users to create and store wiring harness designs.

- All designs and related data belong entirely to the user  
- Designs are private by default and are not visible to other users  
- Sharing features may be introduced in the future, at which point this policy will be updated  

We may access user designs manually for support, debugging, and technical analysis.

## 3. How information is collected
Information is collected when you:
- Create an account  
- Create or modify harness designs  
- Contact us  
- Use the service  
- Interact with the website through cookies or analytics tools  

## 4. Why we collect information
We collect information to:
- Provide and operate the service  
- Store and display user designs  
- Improve functionality and reliability  
- Provide support and troubleshoot issues  
- Maintain security and prevent misuse  
- Comply with legal obligations  

## 5. Legal basis
Where applicable under international law, information is processed based on:
- User consent  
- Performance of a service requested by the user  
- Legitimate interests such as improving the product and maintaining security  

## 6. Cookies, analytics, and ads tracking
We may use cookies and similar technologies for:
- Authentication  
- Basic analytics  
- Performance monitoring  

We also use **Google Ads conversion tracking** to measure the effectiveness of advertising campaigns. This tracking collects only anonymized event data, such as when a user completes a sign-up or other actions related to our ads. No personal identifiers, such as names or email addresses, are collected through this tracking.  

Currently, users are not notified of this tracking and cannot opt out via the site, but you can control cookies and other tracking through your browser settings.

## 7. Data storage and security
Data is stored using Supabase infrastructure located in the United States.

Reasonable technical and organisational measures are taken to protect information from loss, misuse, or unauthorised access.  

No system is completely secure and absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

## 8. Sharing of information
We do not sell personal information.

Information may be shared only with:
- Infrastructure and service providers required to operate the platform  
- Legal authorities if required by law  

No third parties are given access to user designs beyond what is required to operate the service.

## 9. International users
Your information may be processed outside your country of residence, including in the United States. By using the service, you consent to this transfer.

## 10. User rights
Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

### European Union (GDPR)
- Access your personal information  
- Correct inaccuracies  
- Request deletion of your information  
- Withdraw consent for data processing  
- Request data portability  

### California (CCPA)
- Access categories of personal information collected  
- Know the purposes and sources of information collection  
- Request deletion of personal information  
- “Do Not Sell My Information”: we do not sell personal information  

**How to exercise rights:**  
- Users may delete designs directly within the service  
- Account deletion or other requests can be made by emailing:  
**harness.design.nz@gmail.com**

## 11. Data retention
User data is retained only as long as necessary to provide the service.

System backups are retained for up to 24 hours.

## 12. Children
The service is not intended for users under 13 years of age.

This policy may be updated from time to time. Material changes will be posted on this page.
