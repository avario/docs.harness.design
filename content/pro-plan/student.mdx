## How it works

A coupon code will be emailed to your supplied university email address after review. Applications are manually reviewed and may take up to **5 business days**.

## Terms

- This discount is available **once per person**
- The discount provides **$19 USD off the Pro Plan monthly subscription for 12 months**
- The coupon must be used on the **monthly plan only** (not annual)
- After 12 months, billing continues at the standard full price unless cancelled
- You can cancel at any time and access continues until the end of the billing period
- Coupons are personal, single use, and may be revoked if misused or if eligibility cannot be verified
- Coupons expire **1 month after being emailed**
- This discount cannot be combined with any other coupons, discounts, or promotional offers
