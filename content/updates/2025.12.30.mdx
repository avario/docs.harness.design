# Release - 30.12.2025

We just updated the documentation for the new features that have been released recently!

### Mating

You can now mate Connectors and Terminals
https://docs.harness.design/feature-mates

### Groups

Components can be grouped
https://docs.harness.design/components/component-group

### Devices

Device parts can be made and assigned to groups
https://docs.harness.design/parts/part-device

### Net Labels

You can now add net labels to your schematics
https://docs.harness.design/components/component-net-label

### Connector Accessories
The following connector accessories are now available as parts to add to your designs:
  - Lock Part: https://docs.harness.design/parts/connector/part-lock
  - Dust Cover Part: https://docs.harness.design/parts/connector/part-dust-cover
  - Boot Part: https://docs.harness.design/parts/connector/part-boot
  - Backshell Part: https://docs.harness.design/parts/connector/part-backshell
