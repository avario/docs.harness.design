# June 24, 2026

This release adds cavity seal parts for per-contact sealing, and a way to embed an interactive harness on another website.

### Cavity Seals

Cavity seals are a new accessory part for environmental sealing around an individual contact where its wire enters a connector cavity. Like other accessories, a cavity seal is assigned to a [Contact Part](/part/accessory/contact) through a connector configuration, so it's tracked automatically in the BOM.

[Read more...](/part/accessory/cavity-seal)

### Embedding

Once a harness is public, you can embed an interactive copy of it on another website — the same way the live diagram on our home page works. The **Embed** option in the Share dialog lets you choose which views and view options are shown, the color scheme, and how much visitors can interact, then gives you a ready-to-paste `<iframe>` snippet that updates as you change the settings.
