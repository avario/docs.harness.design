# June 9, 2026

This release introduces the 1:1 scale formboard along with a new Enterprise plan.

### Formboard Layout

Formboard mode turns the Layout view into a **1:1 physical scale** drawing of your harness, so you can print full-scale plans to build on or send accurate drawings to a manufacturer. Set bundle lengths and snap components into place to match real-world measurements, see at a glance which bundles aren't to scale, and export each panel as its own to-scale PDF page.

### Enterprise Plan

Formboard is the first feature in our new **Enterprise** plan. The plan page now compares Free, Pro, and Enterprise side by side so you can pick the right fit.

[Read more...](/pro-plan)

### Manual Cavity Designations

Connectors without a part assigned now let you edit cavity designations directly — just click a designation label and type any value. Once a part is assigned, designations are inherited from the part as before.
