# June 19, 2026

This release adds revisions for marking milestones in a harness, and Enterprise teams for collaborating on harnesses together.

### Revisions

Revisions are named snapshots that mark milestones in a harness's history. Create one each time you finish a meaningful piece of work to capture that point, then reopen it later to review it or roll your working version back to it. Creating revisions is a **Pro** feature — on the Free plan you can still view and restore revisions you've already created.

### Teams

Teams let you collaborate on harnesses with colleagues. Create a team from your **Account**, invite members by email, and allocate **Enterprise** seats to the people who need to edit the team's harnesses. The Team tab shows your seat pool, pending invitations, and who's on the team, all managed from one place.

[Read more...](/feature/teams)

### Collaborating on harnesses

Move a harness into your team and every seated member can open and edit it — you keep ownership and full control the whole time. When teammates are in the same harness you can see who else is present, and your view updates automatically when they save, so there's no checking out or refreshing.
