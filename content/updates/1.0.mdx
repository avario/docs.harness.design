# Release 1.0

**harness.design** is no longer in Beta!

We launched the beta at the end of October 2025. Since then, we've been building and refining the tool based on feedback from the community.

With the full release, we've introduced a **Pro plan** to support continued development of the tool.

### <span style={{ color: '#F49F00' }}><IconPro/> Pro Plan</span>

**harness.design** now has a **Pro Plan** for larger harnesses.

The **Free Plan** remains available with unlimited harnesses and parts, but is limited to **100 connections per harness**.

[Read more...](/pro-plan)

### Part Pricing

Parts can now have a **price** assigned. For parts sold by length, a per-unit price can be set. The **Unit Price** and **Total Price** columns in the Parts View show the cost per part and the total cost across the harness.

The currency symbol can be customised under [Harness Settings](/view/main-menu/settings#currency).

### Privacy Policy & Terms of Service

The [Privacy Policy](/legal/privacy-policy) and [Terms of Service](/legal/terms-of-service) have been updated.

Here's a summary of what's changed.

-  Paid plans are now live. harness.design is now free with a Pro upgrade. Free accounts are limited to 100 connections per harness. Pro is $29/month or $288/year (billed annually) for unlimited connections.

-  Our Terms now explicitly state that harness.design is a documentation tool only. All designs should be independently verified for safety and compliance before manufacture or installation.

-  By having an account, you may receive product updates and marketing emails from us. You can unsubscribe at any time using the link in any email.

-  We've named our data processors explicitly — Supabase (storage), Stripe (payments), and Resend (email). NZ users also now have documented rights under the Privacy Act 2020.
