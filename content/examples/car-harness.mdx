## Connections and Parts

The **Connections View** shows corresponding [Wire Parts](/part/wire) from the **Parts View**, **From** and **To** generated from the labels entered into [Components](/component), and wire lengths generated from [Bundle Lengths](/component/bundle#setting-bundle-lengths) in the **Layout View**.

The **Parts View** shows a fully populated list of all parts used in the harness including [Devices](/part/generic), [Connectors](/part/connector), [Wire Parts](/part/wire), and [Contacts](/part/accessory/contact).
