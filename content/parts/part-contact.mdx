## Assigning a Contact Part

Right-click on a connector in the Schematic or Layout View, then use the **Contacts** submenu to assign contact parts to the connector's cavities. You can assign a contact to all cavities or to individual ones.  
<br />
<img src="/components/connector-assign-contact.png" width={634} style={{ borderRadius: '8px' }} />

<Callout>  
You can assign the same contact to multiple connectors by selecting them together (click and drag on the canvas, or use Shift+Click).
</Callout>
