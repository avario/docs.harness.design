# Contact Part
A conductive element installed in a connector housing that forms the electrical interface between mating connectors. This is often crimped or soldered to a wire and has a specific shape to fit into a corresponding connector housing.

## Assigning a Contact to All Cavities

Right-click on the label at the top of the connector and use the **Contacts** submenu to assign the contact part.
<br />
<img src="/parts/part-contact-all.png" width={552} style={{ borderRadius: '8px' }} />
<Callout>  
You can assign the same contact to multiple connectors by selecting them together (click and drag on the canvas, or use Shift+Click).
</Callout>

## Assigning a Contact to Specific Cavities

Right-click the cavity you want to assign, then select the desired contact part.
