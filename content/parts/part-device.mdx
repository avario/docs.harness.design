## Assigning a Device Part

Right-click on the heading of a [Group](/components/component-group) in the Schematic or Layout View and select the device part you want to assign.

## Hiding Parts from BOM
Generally you will not want the components inside a **Device** to be shown in the **BOM**. However you may want to assign parts to the components inside the group so that when you mate a connector or terminal it can validate that this is an appropriate connection. Select the components you wish to exclude from the BOM, right-click, and deselect **Include in BOM**.
