# Connections

The <IconLine/> `Connections` view is a visual representation of the connections in the harness. A connection is any [Wire](/components/component-wire) or [Cable](/components/component-cable) that connects two or more [Components](/components).  This is used to generate the <IconTable/> `Wiring table`.

<br />
<img src="/views/connections.png" width={800} style={{ borderRadius: "8px" }} />

## Table

This is the list of all your **Connections** in the harness.

`right-click` a connection to assign a [Wire Part](/parts/part-wire), add an [Ident](/components/component-wire#assigning-idents) or <IconSwitchHorizontal/> `Switch direction` of the from/to.

Click the <IconArrowDown/> arrow in the column header to sort the table by that column.

See [Keyboard Shortcuts & Mouse Gestures](/shortcuts) for how to navigate the **Connections** view.
