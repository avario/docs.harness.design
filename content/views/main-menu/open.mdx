import { IconFolder, IconUserCircle, IconShare2, IconBraces, IconDotsVertical, IconTrash, IconWorld, IconCircleMinus, IconLockFilled } from "@tabler/icons-react";
import { Callout } from 'nextra/components'

# Open

The <IconFolder/> `Open` panel allows you to open <IconUserCircle/> `Your harnesses` created by you, harnesses <IconShare2/> `Shared with you` or direct from <IconBraces/> `Local data`.

<br />
<img src="/views/open.png" width={600} style={{ borderRadius: "8px" }} />

## <IconUserCircle/> Your harnesses

Harnesses you own.

To open a harness click on the thumbnail of the harness you wish to open.

Click on the <IconDotsVertical/> three dots next to the thumbnail of a harness to <IconShare2/> `Share harness` or <IconTrash/> `Delete harness`.

<Callout> <IconWorld/> `PUBLIC` Will be shown on the thumbnail of any harness that is shared publicly. </Callout>

## <IconShare2/> Shared with you

Harnesses shared with you by other users via email invitation.

To open a harness click on the thumbnail of the harness you wish to open.

Click on the <IconCircleMinus/> `Revoke access` button next to the thumbnail of a harness to revoke your access to the harness. You will need to be re-invited by the owner to regain access to the harness.

<Callout> <IconLockFilled/> `READ_ONLY` Will be shown on the thumbnail of all harnesses that are shared with you. To edit you need to duplicate the harness once open to make your own personal copy. </Callout>

## <IconBraces/> Local data

Open a harness direct from your computers local storage.

`left-click-drag` the raw data file from your computer onto the box to open the harness.

Click here to see how to [export local data](/views/main-menu/export#raw-data)
