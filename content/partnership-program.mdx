# Community Partnership Program

Welcome, and thank you for being part of this. If you're reading this page, it means we've personally invited you to be a harness.design community partner. You're here because you're already an active, valued member of our community, and we'd love to support your work in return.

<Callout>
    This page is private and shared only with community partners. Please don’t share the link publicly.
</Callout>

## What We Ask of You

This program is our way of recognizing the people who are already out there sharing their work, helping others, and building things worth being proud of.

In return for a free Pro license, we'd love it if you shared your work when it feels natural to do so. A build post, a mention in a forum thread, a tag on a photo of your harness. Nothing formal, nothing scripted. Just genuine advocacy from people who actually use the tool.

## Your Pro License

Your Pro license is active for twelve months. We'll check in with you to renew for another year. In practice, if you're active and enjoying the software, renewal is a formality.

## A Note on Disclosure

Even though this is a no-strings-attached arrangement, a lot of countries consider receiving a free product from a brand to be a "material relationship", which means you're legally required to mention it when you promote them. The idea is that your audience deserves to know there's a relationship there, so they can take your recommendation in that context.

Use `#ad` or `#gifted`, or something like `I'm on a free Pro license from harness.design`. Just make sure it's near the top of your post rather than buried in hashtags. If you're making a video, include it as a verbal or on-screen note at the start.

This protects both of us!
