# <IconFolder/>Open

The <IconFolder/>**Open** panel allows you to open <IconUserCircle/>**Your harnesses** created by you, your <IconUsersGroup/>**Team**'s shared harnesses, harnesses <IconShare2/>**Shared with you** or directly from <IconBraces/>**Harness data**.

<br />
<img src="/view/main-menu/open.png" width={600} style={{ borderRadius: "8px" }} />

## <IconUserCircle/>Your harnesses

Harnesses you own.

To open a harness click on the thumbnail of the harness you wish to open.

Click on the <IconDotsVertical/> three dots next to the thumbnail of a harness to:

- <IconPencil />
  **Rename** - Opens a modal to rename the selected harness
- <IconShare2 />
  **Share** - Opens the [Share](/view/main-menu/share) modal
- <IconTrash />
  **Delete** - Deletes the selected harness
- <IconFiles />
  **Duplicate** - Duplicates the selected harness
- <IconUsersGroup />
  **Move to team** - Shares the harness with your [team](/feature/teams) (available to seated team members). See [Collaborating on harnesses](/feature/teams/collaborating).

<Callout>
  <IconWorld /> **PUBLIC** Will be shown on the thumbnail of any harness that is shared publicly.
</Callout>

## <IconUsersGroup/>Team

If you belong to a [team](/feature/teams), a **Team** tab lists every harness shared with your team. Each thumbnail shows the name of the member who owns it.

To open a harness click on its thumbnail.

If you hold a [seat](/feature/teams/seats), use the **New team harness** tile at the end of the grid to create a new harness that's assigned to the team right away.

Click on the <IconDotsVertical/> three dots next to a harness for the actions available to you:

- On harnesses **you own**, you get the same options as in <IconUserCircle/>**Your harnesses**, plus **Remove from team**.
- The **team owner** can **Remove from team** for any of the team's harnesses.

See [Collaborating on harnesses](/feature/teams/collaborating) for how editing a shared team harness works.

## <IconShare2/>Shared with you

Harnesses shared with you by other users via email invitation.

To open a harness click on the thumbnail of the harness you wish to open.

Click on the <IconCircleMinus/>**Revoke access** button next to the thumbnail of a harness to revoke your access to the harness. You will need to be re-invited by the owner to regain access to the harness.

<Callout>
  <IconLock /> **READ ONLY** Will be shown on the thumbnail of all harnesses that are shared with
  you. To edit you need to duplicate the harness once open to make your own personal copy.{" "}
</Callout>

## <IconBraces/>Harness data

Open a harness directly from your computer's local storage.

`left-click-drag` the harness data file from your computer onto the box to open the harness.

[Learn how to export harness data](/view/main-menu/export#-harness-data)
