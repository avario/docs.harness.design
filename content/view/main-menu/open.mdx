# <IconFolder/> Open

The <IconFolder/> `Open` panel allows you to open <IconUserCircle/> `Your harnesses` created by you, harnesses <IconShare2/> `Shared with you` or directly from <IconBraces/> `Local data`.

<br />
<img src="/view/main-menu/open.png" width={600} style={{ borderRadius: "8px" }} />

## <IconUserCircle/> Your harnesses

Harnesses you own.

To open a harness click on the thumbnail of the harness you wish to open.

Click on the <IconDotsVertical/> three dots next to the thumbnail of a harness to:

- <IconPencil /> `Rename` - Opens a modal to rename the selected harness
- <IconShare2 /> `Share` - Opens the [Share](/view/main-menu/share) modal
- <IconTrash /> `Delete` - Deletes the selected harness

<Callout>
  {" "}
  <IconWorld /> `PUBLIC` Will be shown on the thumbnail of any harness that is shared publicly.{" "}
</Callout>

## <IconShare2/> Shared with you

Harnesses shared with you by other users via email invitation.

To open a harness click on the thumbnail of the harness you wish to open.

Click on the <IconCircleMinus/> `Revoke access` button next to the thumbnail of a harness to revoke your access to the harness. You will need to be re-invited by the owner to regain access to the harness.

<Callout>
  {" "}
  <IconLockFilled /> `READ_ONLY` Will be shown on the thumbnail of all harnesses that are shared
  with you. To edit you need to duplicate the harness once open to make your own personal copy.{" "}
</Callout>

## <IconBraces/> Local data

Open a harness directly from your computer's local storage.

`left-click-drag` the raw data file from your computer onto the box to open the harness.

[Learn how to export local data](/view/main-menu/export#raw-data)
