# <IconUserCircle/>Account

Open <IconMenu2/>**Main Menu** → <IconUserCircle/>**Account**.

## <IconUserCircle/>User

Shows your account details — the email address you signed in with, how you signed in (email, Google, or Apple), and your current plan.

## <IconId/>Profile

Set the **display name** shown to other members of your team when you collaborate on a harness. It's also used to label the [revisions](/feature/revisions) you create.

## <IconLock/>Security

Change your account password. If you signed in with Google or Apple, manage your password through that provider instead.

## <IconCreditCard/>Subscription

Shows your current plan and lets you **manage your subscription** or upgrade. If you also hold a team [Enterprise seat](/feature/teams/seats), this tab points out when a personal plan is redundant. See [Plans](/pro-plan) for what each plan includes.

## <IconUsersGroup/>Team

Create or manage your [team](/feature/teams) — invite members, allocate [seats](/feature/teams/seats), and see who belongs to the team. See [Teams](/feature/teams) for the full guide.

## Logout & account actions

At the bottom of the tab list:

- <IconLogout />
  **Logout** - Signs you out of your account.
- **Terms of Service** / **Privacy Policy** - Open the legal documents.
- **Delete account** - Permanently delete your harness.design account.
