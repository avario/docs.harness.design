# <IconLine/>Connections

The <IconLine/>`Connections` view is a visual representation of the connections in the harness. A connection is any [Wire](/component/wire) or [Cable](/component/cable) that connects two or more [Components](/component).  This is used to generate the <IconTable/>`Wiring table`.

<br />
<img src="/view/connections.png" width={800} style={{ borderRadius: "8px" }} />

## Table

This is the list of all your **Connections** in the harness.

`right-click` a connection to assign a [Wire Part](/part/wire), add an [Ident](/component/wire#assigning-idents) or <IconSwitchHorizontal/>`Switch direction` of the from/to.

Click the <IconArrowDown/> arrow in the column header to sort the table by that column.

## Assign Connections

Type in the to or from to change the assigned location of a connection.
